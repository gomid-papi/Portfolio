About Me

Hello, and welcome to my creative corner on the web! My name is Owen Leigh Gomid, and I am currently pursuing a degree in BS-Animation and Multimedia Arts at the Northwestern Mindanao State College of Science and Technology (NMSCST). I am a passionate artist and aspiring animator, driven by a love for art, storytelling, and bringing ideas to life.

Here are some of the creative fields I specialize in:

  • Animations: Bringing stories to life with motion and imagination.
  • Digital Art: Crafting vibrant and detailed pieces using modern tools.
  • Graphic Design: Designing visuals for branding, events, and marketing materials.
  • Portraits: Capturing the unique essence of individuals through traditional and digital mediums.

Every piece I create reflects a part of who I am, making them meaningful and unique. Whether it’s a special gift for a loved one or a piece to cherish for yourself, my art is crafted with care and passion. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see the stories I’ve brought to life. Thank you for visiting, and I hope my creations inspire and resonate with you!
